# Privacy Policy for TheMind

**Effective Date:** March 24, 2026

Your privacy is a core value of TheMind. We designed this app with the fundamental belief that your thoughts, notes, audio recordings, images, and saved links are your private property. 

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use TheMind ("the App").

### 1. Data Collection & Storage (We Don't Collect Your Data)
TheMind operates entirely on your personal device. 
*   **No Accounts:** You do not need an account to use the App.
*   **No Servers:** We do not have servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that intercept, read, or store your thoughts. 
*   **Local Storage:** All text, audio files, images, tags, and link metadata you capture are stored securely on your iOS device using Apple’s native local storage frameworks. 

### 2. Audio & Speech Recognition
If you use the audio recording feature, TheMind requests access to your microphone and Apple's Speech Recognition service. 
*   The audio you record is saved locally on your device.
*   Speech recognition is processed by Apple's native APIs. We do not transmit, analyze, or process your voice or transcriptions on our own servers. For details on how Apple handles data processed by these APIs, please refer to Apple’s Privacy Policy.

### 3. Share Extension & Link Previews
When you use the Share Extension to save a link from Safari, Instagram, or other apps:
*   TheMind connects briefly to the target website to fetch publicly available metadata (like the site title, domain, and an image thumbnail) to generate a rich preview.
*   This connection happens directly from your device to the website. We do not track the URLs you save, nor do we run analytics on your saved links.

### 4. iCloud Sync (If Enabled)
If you have iCloud enabled on your device, Apple may backup your app data (including your thoughts and media) to your personal iCloud account so you can restore it if you lose your device. This happens exclusively through your encrypted Apple ID. We have no access to your iCloud data.

### 5. Third-Party Analytics and Tracking
We do **not** use any third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or Facebook Pixel). We do **not** track your behavior, log your usage, or sell your data to advertisers. 

### 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this policy if we introduce new features (such as opt-in syncing). If we do, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the app constitutes your agreement to any changes.
